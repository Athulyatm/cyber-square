Cybersquare learning platform

Every registered student will have a separate login account to the Cybersquare online learning platform. Here,they will be able to view their course progress,assignments provided by teachers,their batch performance,progress cards,and so on. They will be able to attent online exams and live classes. Students can attent live classes, to clear their doubts and interact with teachers. Apart from these, they will also get notifications of upcoming live classes,news, and events so that they wont miss them.

Teachers can also login into Cybersquare platform and able to view the students performance, batch progress, make reports, evaluate their works such as assignments, projects, questions etc.Teachers can sehedule an online class and online exams also.
